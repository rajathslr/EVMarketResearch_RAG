**EVgo AutoCharge Program Explanation**  
- **AutoCharge program:** Enables qualifying EVs to automatically start charging simply by plugging in at any EVgo station, eliminating the need to interact with station menus or the app during charging sessions.  
- To enable AutoCharge: Open EVgo app → Home menu → Tap Vehicles → Select AutoCharge → Add vehicle by search or VIN. Once set up, users “plug in and walk away.”

### **Key Insights**  
- **EVgo’s AutoCharge program** streamlines charging for compatible vehicles but is currently unavailable for Volkswagen ID.4.  
- New EVgo stations are emerging in urban Southern California but suffer early-stage operational and maintenance issues (offline chargers, cable reach problems).  
- EVgo stations are located more in urban in-fill locations rather than along freeways, offering different use case advantages compared to Electrify America.  
- Charging session performance can vary, but recent sessions show efficient power delivery at about 100–120 kW for the ID.4.  
- EVgo's app provides extensive real-time charging data preferred by some users over Electrify America’s app, though post-session summaries are limited.  
- Parking layout, especially regarding handicap spots, can pose conflicts for EV users and disabled drivers, underscoring the need for considerate station design.  
- Charging session limits (60 minutes max) may impact slower-charging vehicles.
