[00:00:03] **Introduction to the EVgo App**  
The EVgo app is a mobile application designed for electric vehicle (EV) owners aiming for convenient and efficient charging solutions. It connects users to one of the largest fast public charging networks in the United States, which includes over **1,100 stations across more than 40 states**. The app supports both **iOS and Android** platforms, ensuring broad accessibility for EV drivers.

- **Core purpose:** Quickly locate and access EV charging stations.
- **Network scale:** 1,100+ charging stations, spanning 40+ U.S. states.
- **Platform compatibility:** iOS and Android devices.

[00:00:38] **Key Features: Station Locator and Status Indicators**  
A prominent function of the EVgo app is the ability to locate nearby charging stations easily. It displays **real-time availability** and provides **turn-by-turn navigation** to chosen stations. The app incorporates a clear **color-coded system** to communicate charger status:

| Color  | Charger Status  | Meaning                      |
|--------|-----------------|------------------------------|
| Green  | Available       | Ready for use                |
| Red    | Busy           | Currently occupied           |
| Gray   | Offline         | Not operational/unavailable |

This real-time availability data is crucial for efficient trip planning, minimizing downtime and wait times at stations.

[00:01:12] **Charging Initiation and AutoCharge Plus**  
Users can initiate charging sessions directly through their phones, negating the need for physical access cards or RFID tags. The app extends convenience with a feature called **AutoCharge Plus**, which enables compatible EVs to start charging automatically when plugged in, without any manual interaction.

- **AutoCharge Plus:** Automatic charging start with no app or card interaction.
- Benefits: Speeds up the charging process and simplifies user experience.

[00:01:43] **Network Roaming and Station Filtering**  
The EVgo network is not limited to its proprietary stations; it also integrates with partner networks through roaming agreements, including ChargePoint. This expands the coverage and options available for charging.

Users can filter stations based on important technical criteria to ensure compatibility and charging efficiency:

| Filter Type       | Options/Examples                          |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Connector Type    | North American Combo Standard (CCS), Kodamo, Combined Charging System (CCS) |
