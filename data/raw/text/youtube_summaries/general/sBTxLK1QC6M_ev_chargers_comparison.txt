**Installation recommendations and safety warnings:**

- Hardwired charger installations are strongly recommended over plug-in units due to safety concerns.
- Many plug-in chargers lack temperature sensors in the plug, and low-quality NEMA 1450 outlets (often installed cheaply) have melted under continuous EV charging loads, leading to potential fire hazards.
- High-quality industrial-grade NEMA 1450 outlets cost $50-$60 and should be used by licensed electricians.
- The presenter has documented multiple user issues via the "Recharge Rescue" video series primarily caused by inferior outlet installations.
- Homeowners should always hire licensed electricians, pull permits, and have installations inspected to ensure safety and reliability.
