**Downloading and Setting Up the Tesla App**  
- Users can find the app by searching **“Tesla”** in either the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.  
- The official Tesla app is the first result and is **free to download**.  
- Upon opening the app, users must either **sign in** or **create a new Tesla account**.  
- Account creation requires filling in necessary information followed by enabling **notifications**, which are essential for receiving alerts from the app.  
- To add your Tesla to the app:  
  - Tap the **person icon** in the top right, then select **add products**  
  - Required for setup: the Tesla’s **VIN**, internet access (Wi-Fi or LTE), **driver’s license**, and **proof of ownership or business documents** for company-owned vehicles  
  - Adding the car to the app may take **3 to 5 days** but sometimes happens instantly.

**Setting Up Phone Key and Initial Connectivity**  
- Users can set up their phone as a **wireless key** enabling them to unlock and drive their Tesla without pulling out the physical key card.  
- Process involves:  
  - Selecting **setup phone key** from the main menu  
  - Allowing Tesla access to device location  
  - Pairing by placing the physical key card on the Tesla’s center console  
- Once paired, the phone key enables **automatic unlocking on approach** and **automatic locking upon departure**.
