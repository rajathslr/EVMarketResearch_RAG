### Glossary of Key Terms:  

| Term                  | Definition                                                                                      |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Level 1 Charging      | Slow charging using standard household outlets (e.g., 120V in North America).                  |
| Level 2 Charging      | Faster charging using 240V outlets or dedicated EV charging stations.                           |
| Level 3 Charging (DC Fast Charging) | High-power DC charging, typically >50 kW, for rapid recharging of EV batteries.               |
| CCS Combo Plug        | A common fast-charging plug standard compatible with many non-Tesla EVs.                     |
| NEMA 1450 Plug        | A type of high-amperage outlet often used for portable Level 2 charging equipment.             |
| Regenerative Braking  | Technology allowing the vehicle to recapture energy when slowing down or descending hills.     |
