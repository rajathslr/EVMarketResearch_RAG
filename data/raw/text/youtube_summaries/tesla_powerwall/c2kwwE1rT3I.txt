- App uses **time-scale filters** (day, week, month) to view energy use and costs.

- Graphs visualize costs offset by solar generation and Powerwall discharge to maximize self-consumption.

### Powerwall Settings and Backup Reserve
- Users can set a **Powerwall backup reserve percentage** (example: 20%).
- The backup portion is reserved to maintain power in case of an outage.
- The remainder (e.g., 80%) is available for everyday use.
- Users can adjust the reserve anywhere between 0% to 100%, balancing backup reliability vs. usable capacity.

### Operational Modes
| Mode            | Description                                                                                             | Best For                                   |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Self-Powered    | Powerwall does not draw energy from the grid; solely uses solar and stored energy.                    | Users without cheap night rates; solar maximizers.|
| Time-Based Control | Powerwall follows utility rates to charge during cheap off-peak hours and discharge during peak rates.| Users with cheap night tariffs or EV charging schedules.|

- Most users prefer **time-based control** to optimize costs by leveraging low tariff windows.

### Utility Rate Plan Configuration
- Users can select and customize their **utility provider and rate plans** in the app.
- Example given: Octopus Go tariff with a 5-hour cheap night period.
- Supports entering **seasonal tariffs** allowing multiple rate windows annually (e.g., switching between EV and heat pump plans in different seasons).
- Customizable daytime and nighttime tariff periods with draggable sliders for fine-tuning.

### Powerwall Algorithm and Learning Capabilities
- Powerwall undergoes a **2–3 month learning phase** adapting to household usage patterns.
- The system predicts events like heavy usage days (e.g., baking or family visits) and pre-charges accordingly during cheap rates.
- Optimizes charge/discharge around expected solar conditions, weather forecasts, and tariff schedules to minimize costs.

### Pricing and Selling Energy Back to Grid
- Users can input exact values for:
- Off-peak purchase price (example: 16p commercial, modified to 8p domestic rate).
- Sell-back price (e.g., 0.8p/kWh).
- Peak rates (example: 21p/kWh).
- The Powerwall can **purchase energy in advance** to avoid super-peak periods by slightly overpaying earlier during cheaper but still off-peak hours.
- Settings must be saved manually after editing rates.
