**Off-Grid Mode Feature**
- A rarely used but notable feature is the ability to go off-grid manually from the app.
- When off-grid mode is activated (typically with local connection), the Powerwall disconnects from the grid supply and powers the home solely with battery storage and solar energy.
- Users often activate this to:
- Prepare for known outages by testing off-grid functionality
- Demonstrate system capabilities to visitors
- Continuous off-grid operation is possible but generally recommended only for testing or outages.

**Settings and Customizations (Core Functionalities)**
- This section contains vital controls for optimizing Powerwall use:

| Setting                      | Description                                                                                                   | Notes / Recommendations                                                          |
|------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Backup Reserve Level         | Adjustable percentage (0%-100%) of battery capacity set aside for backup power in outages                     | Recommended 20% if frequent outages; full saving if outages rare                 |
| Operational Modes            | - Self-Powered: battery prioritizes solar, no grid draw<br>- Time-Based Control: blends solar, grid, tariffs  | Time-Based Control is used to automate charging based on tariffs and export rates |
| Utility Rate Plan Setup      | Define peak/off-peak/mid-peak/super off-peak time periods, prices for each                                        | Supports complex tariff schemes including seasonality if applicable              |
| Permission to Export         | Enables export of electricity and associated credits                                                            | Must be enabled to sell excess solar power                                      |
| Grid Charging Enable/Disable | Allows or disallows Powerwall charging from grid power                                                           | Disabling prevents Powerwall from charging on cheap grid rates                   |
| Storm Watch                  | Connects to Met Office weather warnings (yellow/red)                                                           | Automatically charges Powerwall preemptively for outages and sends push notifications|
| Manage Users                | Add or remove access for multiple users                                                                          | Supports household or facility sharing                                           |
| Support and Manuals         | Access to tutorials, manuals, and troubleshooting resources                                                      | Provides additional help for users                                              |

- Users can precisely configure off-peak schedules and prices; example: Octopus Go with 5 hours off-peak at a discounted rate.
- *Important Note*: Powerwall 3 cannot detect complex time-of-use plans such as Octopus Go Intelligent's cheap charging slots without third-party integrations (e.g., Home Assistant or Net Zero app).
